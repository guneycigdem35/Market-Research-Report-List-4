<p><p>Agricultural tires can be categorized into two main types: Radial Agriculture Tires and Bias (Crossply) Agriculture Tires. </p><p>Radial agriculture tires are known for their durability, improved traction, and longer tread life. They have a flexible sidewall construction which allows for better performance on various terrains.</p><p>Bias or crossply agriculture tires have a stiffer sidewall construction and are generally more affordable than radial tires. While they may not offer the same level of performance as radials, they are suitable for certain applications and are still widely used in the agricultural industry.</p></p>

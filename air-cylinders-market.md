<p><p>Air cylinders are essential components in various industries, such as manufacturing and automotive. Single-acting cylinders operate in one direction, relying on compressed air to extend or retract the piston. On the other hand, double-acting cylinders use compressed air to move the piston in both directions. Single-acting cylinders are ideal for applications where only one direction of force is needed, while double-acting cylinders are commonly used for tasks requiring force in both directions. Both types play a crucial role in automation and industrial processes.</p></p>

<p><p>Alternating Current Micro Motors are classified into two main types: Single Phase and Three Phase markets. Single Phase motors are simpler and more commonly used in household appliances and small industrial applications. They have a single voltage waveform that varies in amplitude over time. Three Phase motors are more efficient and powerful, commonly used in larger industrial settings. They have three voltage waveforms that are out of phase with each other, providing smoother and more consistent power delivery.</p></p>

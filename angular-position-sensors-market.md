<p><p>The Angular Position Sensors market is categorized into two main types: linear angular position sensors and rotary angular position sensors. Linear angular position sensors measure the angular displacement along a linear path, converting the position into an electrical signal, ideal for applications in automation and robotics. In contrast, rotary angular position sensors are designed to detect angular rotation, commonly used in motors, servos, and various industrial applications. Both types play critical roles in enhancing precision and control in various technological systems.</p></p>

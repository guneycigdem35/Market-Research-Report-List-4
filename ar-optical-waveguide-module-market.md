<p><strong>The AR Optical Waveguide Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective Waveguides</li><li>Diffractive Waveguides</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AR Optical Waveguide Module market can be segmented into three main types: Reflective Waveguides, Diffractive Waveguides, and Others. Reflective waveguides use mirrors to reflect light within the waveguide, allowing for a compact and lightweight design. Diffractive waveguides use diffraction gratings to guide light, offering a wider field of view and potentially higher resolution. The "Others" category includes emerging technologies that do not fall specifically into the reflective or diffractive waveguide categories, such as holographic waveguides.</p></p>

<p><p>Asthma drugs market includes quick-relief medications, also known as rescue medications, which are used to provide immediate relief during asthma attacks. These medications help to relax and open the airways quickly to alleviate symptoms. On the other hand, medications for allergy-induced asthma target the underlying allergic response that triggers asthma symptoms. These medications help to reduce inflammation and prevent the airways from becoming inflamed in response to allergens, thus reducing asthma symptoms in the long term.</p></p>

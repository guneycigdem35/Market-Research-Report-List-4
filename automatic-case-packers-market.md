<p><p>Automatic case packers come in two main types - semi-automatic and fully automatic. Semi-automatic case packers require some manual intervention in the packaging process, such as manually placing products into the machine. They are ideal for smaller production volumes and can offer flexibility in packaging various types of products. Fully automatic case packers, on the other hand, require minimal manual labor as they can automatically pack products into cases. They are suitable for high-volume production environments and can significantly increase packaging efficiency.</p></p>

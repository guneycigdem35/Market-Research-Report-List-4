<p><p>Automatic endoscope cleaners are divided into two main types: single chamber cleaners and multi-chamber cleaners. Single chamber cleaners are designed for cleaning a single endoscope at a time, making them suitable for smaller facilities with lower throughput. On the other hand, multi-chamber cleaners are capable of cleaning multiple endoscopes simultaneously, increasing efficiency for larger healthcare facilities with higher demand. Both types of cleaners offer automated cleaning processes, ensuring thorough and consistent cleaning of endoscopes to maintain patient safety and equipment longevity.</p></p>

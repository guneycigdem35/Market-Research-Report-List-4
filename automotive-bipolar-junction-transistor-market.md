<p><ul><li>NPN Transistor</li><li>PNP Transistor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bipolar junction transistors (BJTs) in the automotive market can be categorized into two types: NPN transistors and PNP transistors. NPN transistors have a structure where a layer of P-doped semiconductor is sandwiched between two layers of N-doped semiconductor, while PNP transistors have the opposite structure. Both types of transistors are widely used in automotive electronics for functions such as amplification, switching, and regulation of electrical signals. They play a crucial role in ensuring proper operation and efficiency of various systems in vehicles.</p></p>

<p><p>Automotive ignition coils are essential components in a vehicle's ignition system, responsible for converting low voltage power from the battery into high voltage power needed to ignite the spark plugs. The main types of automotive ignition coils include can-type ignition coils, pencil ignition coils, double spark coils, and other unique variations. Can-type coils are traditional and often used in older vehicles, while pencil coils are more compact and efficient. Double spark coils feature two spark plugs per coil, providing improved ignition performance. Other variations offer unique designs and capabilities to suit different vehicle requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>Automotive window voltage supervisors are devices that monitor voltage levels in car windows to ensure they operate properly. Single-channel supervisors monitor one window at a time, while multi-channel supervisors can monitor multiple windows simultaneously. Single-channel supervisors are more basic and cost-effective, ideal for smaller vehicles with fewer windows. Multi-channel supervisors are more advanced and efficient, suitable for larger vehicles with multiple windows. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the smooth operation of car windows.</p></p>

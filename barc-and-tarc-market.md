<p><p>BARC (Bottom Anti-Reflection Coatings) and TARC (Top Anti-Reflection Coatings) are essential components in the semiconductor and photovoltaic industries. BARC is applied beneath photoresist layers to minimize reflection, enhancing pattern transfer during lithography. Conversely, TARC is used on top of the photoresist to reduce reflections that can distort imaging. Both coatings improve the performance and efficiency of devices by ensuring precise optical properties, ultimately contributing to higher yields in manufacturing processes and enhancing the efficiency of solar cells.</p></p>

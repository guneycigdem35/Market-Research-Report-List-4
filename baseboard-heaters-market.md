<p><ul><li>Electric Type</li><li>Hydronic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Baseboard heaters are categorized into two main types: electric and hydronic. Electric baseboard heaters use electricity to heat a heating element, which then warms the surrounding air. These heaters are easy to install and maintain, making them a popular choice for many households. On the other hand, hydronic baseboard heaters use heated water or oil to provide radiant heat. These heaters are known for their energy efficiency and ability to provide consistent, even heat throughout a room. Both types have their own advantages and cater to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>

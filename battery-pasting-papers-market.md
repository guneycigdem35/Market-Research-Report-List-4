<p><ul><li>Cellulose Pasting Papers</li><li>Fiberglass Pasting Papers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The battery pasting papers market consists of two primary types: cellulose pasting papers and fiberglass pasting papers. Cellulose pasting papers are made from natural materials, offering good absorbency and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for lead-acid battery applications. In contrast, fiberglass pasting papers are known for their high strength and durability, providing enhanced performance and thermal resistance, ideal for high-performance batteries. Both types play crucial roles in the manufacturing process, affecting the efficiency and longevity of battery products.</p></p>

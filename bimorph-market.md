<p><p>Bimorph market consists of two main types: Piezoelectric Bimorph and Bimetal Bimorph. Piezoelectric Bimorphs use the piezoelectric effect to convert electrical energy into mechanical motion, while Bimetal Bimorphs utilize the different thermal expansion rates of two materials bonded together to generate movement. Both types are commonly used in various applications such as sensors, actuators, and switches due to their ability to efficiently convert energy and produce precise movements.</p></p>

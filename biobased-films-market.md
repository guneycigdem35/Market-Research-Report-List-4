<p><p>Biobased films are categorized into various types including PBAT films, PLA films, PHB films, and Others market. PBAT films are made from a blend of bio-based materials and petroleum-based materials, offering flexibility and strength. PLA films are derived from plant sources like corn starch and are biodegradable and compostable. PHB films are made from microbial fermentation and are fully biodegradable. The Others market includes biodegradable films made from various sources such as starch, cellulose, and proteins, providing environmentally friendly packaging solutions.</p></p>

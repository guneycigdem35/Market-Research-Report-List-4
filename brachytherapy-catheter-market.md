There are two main types of brachytherapy catheters: Single Lumen Balloon Catheters (SLB) and Multi Lumen Balloon Catheters (MLB). SLBs have one central channel for radiation delivery, while MLBs have multiple channels for more precise radiation targeting. Both types of catheters are used in various cancer treatments to minimize harm to surrounding tissues and improve treatment outcomes.</p></p>

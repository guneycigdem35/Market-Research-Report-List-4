<p><p>The briquette industry offers various market types, including Solid Cylindrical, RUF, and Pini-Kay briquettes. Solid Cylindrical briquettes are uniform in shape and density, ideal for industrial applications. RUF briquettes are known for their high density and efficiency, often produced using a hydraulic press, making them suitable for both domestic and commercial use. Pini-Kay briquettes have a distinctive oval shape with a hollow center, enhancing combustion efficiency and air flow, making them popular among users seeking effective biomass fuel options.</p></p>

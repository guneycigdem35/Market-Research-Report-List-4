<p><ul><li>Smooth Bottom</li><li>Net Bottom</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Buccal tubes in orthodontics are available in two main market types: smooth bottom and net bottom. Smooth bottom buccal tubes have a flat surface on the bottom, which allows for easy placement and a secure bond with the tooth. On the other hand, net bottom buccal tubes have a textured surface on the bottom, providing increased retention for the adhesive. Both types offer reliable attachment of archwires and other orthodontic components, but the choice between them may depend on the specific needs and preferences of the orthodontist and patient.</p></p>

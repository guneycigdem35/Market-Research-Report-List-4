<p><p>The cardiac rhythm management systems market encompasses several key types: Heart Failure (HF) management systems focus on monitoring and optimizing heart function in patients with heart failure. Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) devices improve the heart's efficiency by coordinating contractions. Implantable Cardiac Defibrillators (ICDs) deliver electric shocks to restore normal rhythm during life-threatening arrhythmias. Pacemakers regulate heartbeats in patients with bradycardia, ensuring adequate heart rate. Together, these devices enhance patient outcomes and quality of life for individuals with cardiac conditions.</p></p>

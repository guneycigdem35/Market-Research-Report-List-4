<p><p>Cartridge heaters are heating elements used in a wide range of industrial applications. There are two main types of cartridge heaters: standard cartridge heaters and swaged cartridge heaters. Standard cartridge heaters are designed with a more uniform watt density along the length of the heater, making them suitable for general heating applications. On the other hand, swaged cartridge heaters have a higher watt density at the tip, providing more concentrated heat transfer for applications requiring higher temperatures or faster heating rates. Both types offer reliable and efficient heating solutions for various industries.</p></p>

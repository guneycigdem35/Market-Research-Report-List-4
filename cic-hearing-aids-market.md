<p><p>CIC (Completely-in-Canal) hearing aids are available in two main types: Digital Signal Processing (DSP) and Simulated Signal Processing. DSP hearing aids use advanced technology to process sound signals digitally, providing improved clarity and customization for the user. On the other hand, Simulated Signal Processing hearing aids mimic the processing done by the brain to adjust and enhance sound levels. Both types offer discreet, comfortable, and effective solutions for individuals with hearing loss.</p></p>

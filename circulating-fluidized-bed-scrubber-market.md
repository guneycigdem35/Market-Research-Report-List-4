<p><p>Circulating Fluidized Bed Scrubber market is divided into two main types - dry and semi-dry. Dry scrubbers use dry sorbent substances to remove pollutants from the gas stream, while semi-dry scrubbers use a combination of dry and wet processes. Dry scrubbers are more cost-effective and simpler to operate, but may not be as efficient in removing certain pollutants. Semi-dry scrubbers offer a balance between cost and effectiveness by utilizing both methods. Both types play a crucial role in reducing emissions and improving air quality in various industries.</p></p>

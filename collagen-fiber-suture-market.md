<p><ul><li>Monofilament</li><li>Multifilament</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Collagen Fiber Suture Market includes two main types: monofilament and multifilament. Monofilament sutures are single-strand threads, which are smooth and reduce tissue trauma. They are less likely to harbor bacteria and are easy to handle. In contrast, multifilament sutures are braided strands, offering higher tensile strength and knot security. They are flexible and easy to manipulate. Both types are biodegradable and provide excellent support during wound healing processes, making them popular choices in surgical procedures.</p></p>

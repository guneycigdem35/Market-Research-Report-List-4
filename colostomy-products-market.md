<p><p>Colostomy products market can be categorized into two main types: one-piece bags and two-piece bags. One-piece bags consist of a single unit where the adhesive wafer and collection bag are connected. They are compact and easy to use. On the other hand, two-piece bags have a separate adhesive wafer and collection bag, allowing for easy changing and customization. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of individuals with colostomies, providing options for comfort and convenience.</p></p>

<p><p>The commercial insulation market is divided into several key types: wraps, coatings, adhesives, and sealants. Wraps are used to cover and insulate pipes and ducts, providing thermal efficiency and moisture resistance. Coatings are protective layers applied to surfaces for added insulation and energy efficiency. Adhesives facilitate secure bonding of insulation materials, ensuring proper installation and performance. Sealants are used to fill gaps and joints, preventing air leaks and contributing to overall energy conservation in commercial buildings.</p></p>

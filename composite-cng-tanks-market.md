<p><p>Composite CNG tanks are used in vehicles powered by compressed natural gas. Two main types are glass fiber composites and carbon fiber composites. Glass fiber composites are cost-effective and have high impact resistance, making them suitable for light-duty vehicles. Carbon fiber composites are lightweight and offer high strength-to-weight ratio, making them ideal for high-performance vehicles. Both types of composites are popular choices in the automotive industry for their durability and efficiency in storing CNG.</p></p>

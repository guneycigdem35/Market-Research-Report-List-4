<p><p>Connected car devices market types include Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V), Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I), and Vehicle-to-Pedestrian (V2P). V2V technology enables communication between vehicles to enhance safety and efficiency on the road. V2I technology allows vehicles to communicate with infrastructure such as traffic lights and road signs for improved traffic management. V2P technology enables vehicles to communicate with pedestrians through wearable devices or smartphones to prevent accidents and ensure pedestrian safety. These technologies collectively contribute to creating a smarter and safer transportation network.</p></p>

<p><p>Crosslinked Polyethylene (PE-X) Pipe Market includes three major types: PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc. PE-Xa pipes are produced using the peroxide method, offering excellent high-temperature resistance and flexibility. PE-Xb pipes are manufactured through a silane method, providing a balance between cost and performance. PE-Xc pipes are created using the electron beam method, offering superior chemical resistance. Each type has its unique characteristics and applications, catering to different needs in industries such as plumbing, heating, and cooling systems.</p></p>

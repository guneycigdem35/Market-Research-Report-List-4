<p><p>The cylinder head gasket market is segmented into three primary types: metal gaskets, non-metal gaskets, and composite material gaskets. Metal gaskets, often made from materials like aluminum or copper, offer superior heat resistance and durability, suitable for high-performance engines. Non-metal gaskets, typically rubber or plastic-based, are lightweight and provide good sealing in lower-stress environments. Composite material gaskets combine multiple materials, offering a balance of strength, flexibility, and thermal stability, making them versatile for various automotive applications.</p></p>

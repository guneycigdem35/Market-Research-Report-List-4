<p><p>Cylindrical robots come in different types based on the number of degrees of freedom they offer. Three Degree of Freedom (3-DOF) robots have three joints that allow them to move in three directions. Multi Degree of Freedom (DOF) robots have more than three joints, offering greater flexibility and maneuverability in various tasks. Both types cater to different market needs, with 3-DOF robots being more suitable for simpler applications, while Multi-DOF robots are used for more complex and versatile tasks.</p></p>

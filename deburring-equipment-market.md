<p><p>Deburring equipment can be divided into two main types: Hand deburring equipment and automatic deburring equipment. Hand deburring equipment is manually operated and requires the user to physically remove burrs from a workpiece. On the other hand, automatic deburring equipment is machine-driven and can perform deburring processes automatically without human intervention. Both types of equipment are used in various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace to ensure smooth and clean surfaces on metal, plastic, and other materials.</p></p>

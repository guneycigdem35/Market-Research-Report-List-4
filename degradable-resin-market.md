<p><p>Degradable resin market types include PLA (polylactic acid), PBS (polybutylene succinate), PBTA (polybutylene terephthalate), and others. PLA is a biodegradable polymer made from renewable resources like corn starch. PBS is a biodegradable polyester often used in packaging and agricultural applications. PBTA is a biodegradable copolyester that offers high strength and durability. Other degradable resins may include PHA (polyhydroxyalkanoates) and PCL (polycaprolactone). These materials offer eco-friendly alternatives to traditional plastics and are increasingly in demand due to growing environmental concerns.</p></p>

<p><p>Dental scalers are tools used in dental hygiene to remove plaque and calculus from teeth. There are two main types of dental scalers: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric. Magnetostrictive dental scalers use magnetic fields to create ultrasonic vibrations in a metal stack, while piezoelectric dental scalers utilize crystals that produce ultrasonic vibrations when an electric current is applied. Both types of scalers are effective in removing tartar and maintaining oral health, but they differ in their mechanism of action.</p></p>

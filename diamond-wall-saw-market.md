<p><ul><li>Diamond Continuous Rim Blade</li><li>Diamond Turbine Blade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Diamond Continuous Rim Blade is a type of wall saw blade with a continuous band of diamonds around the circumference, providing a smooth and clean cut. It is commonly used for cutting hard materials such as concrete, brick, and stone. On the other hand, the Diamond Turbine Blade is designed with specially shaped segments to help dissipate heat during cutting, making it ideal for high-speed cutting of abrasive materials like asphalt and green concrete. Both types of blades cater to different cutting needs in the construction industry.</p></p>

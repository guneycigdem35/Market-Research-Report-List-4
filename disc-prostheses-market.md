<p><p>Disc prostheses are medical devices used to replace damaged spinal discs and alleviate pain and restore functionality. There are two main types of disc prostheses: cervical disc and lumbar disc. Cervical disc prostheses are designed to be implanted in the neck region of the spine to treat conditions such as herniated discs or degenerative disc disease. Lumbar disc prostheses are designed for the lower back and can help address issues such as disc degeneration or spinal stenosis. Both types of disc prostheses aim to improve patient's quality of life and mobility.</p></p>

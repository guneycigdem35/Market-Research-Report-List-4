<p><p>Dissolution apparatus is a laboratory instrument used for testing the rate at which a solid substance dissolves in a liquid solution. It is commonly used in pharmaceutical research and development to determine the dissolution rate of drugs and ensure their efficacy. The apparatus consists of a vessel in which the solid substance is placed along with the liquid solution, and a paddle or basket that agitates the solution to simulate the conditions of the human body.</p><p>The Dissolution Apparatus Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.1% during the forecast period.

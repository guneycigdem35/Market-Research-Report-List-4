<p><p>Dry heat sterilizers are equipment used to sterilize medical, dental or laboratory equipment through the use of dry hot air. The dry hot air sterilization method involves heating the equipment at high temperatures to kill bacteria and other microbes. Flame burning sterilization, on the other hand, utilizes an open flame to sterilize equipment. Other types of dry heat sterilizers include methods such as infrared radiation or microwave sterilization. Each method effectively eliminates microorganisms to ensure equipment is safe for use.</p></p>

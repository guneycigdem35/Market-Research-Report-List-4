<p><p>The electric vehicle self-dimming mirror market can be divided into two types: outer self-dimming mirrors and inside self-dimming mirrors. Outer self-dimming mirrors are designed to reduce glare from headlights of vehicles behind the car, enhancing visibility and safety for the driver. Inside self-dimming mirrors work similarly, but are focused on reducing glare from headlights coming from the rear of the vehicle. Both types of self-dimming mirrors use technology to automatically adjust the level of dimming based on the intensity of light.</p></p>

<p><p>Electrotherapy Pain Relief Systems involve the use of various techniques to alleviate pain. The two main types in the market are Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) and Microcurrent Nerve Stimulation (MENS). TENS delivers low-voltage electrical currents through electrode pads placed on the skin, which help block pain signals. MENS, on the other hand, uses even lower levels of electrical currents to stimulate the nerves and promote healing. Both types of electrotherapy systems are non-invasive and can be used at home or under medical supervision for pain management.</p></p>

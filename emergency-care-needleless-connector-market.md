<p><p>Emergency care needleless connectors are devices used to connect catheters, tubing, or syringes without the need for a needle. There are three main market types based on how they function: Positive Fluid Displacement connectors have a valve that opens when connected, allowing fluid to flow. Negative Fluid Displacement connectors have a valve that closes when connected, preventing fluid from leaking. Neutral Displacement connectors have a valve that remains closed until a fluid transfer is initiated, then opens to allow flow. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different medical scenarios.</p></p>

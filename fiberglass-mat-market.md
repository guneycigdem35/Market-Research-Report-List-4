<p><ul><li>Chopped Strand Mat</li><li>Air Laid Mat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiberglass Mat market consists of two main types - Chopped Strand Mat and Air Laid Mat. Chopped Strand Mat is made up of randomly chopped fiberglass strands that are bonded together with a binder. It is commonly used in applications requiring strength and rigidity. On the other hand, Air Laid Mat is made by randomly dispersing fibers in an airstream and then bonding them together. It is used in applications where flexibility and conformability are important. Both types cater to different market needs and applications within the fiberglass industry.</p></p>

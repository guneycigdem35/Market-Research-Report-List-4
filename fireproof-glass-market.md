<p><strong>The Fireproof Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wired</li><li>Ceramic</li><li>Laminated</li><li>Tempered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fireproof glass market comprises wired, ceramic, laminated, and tempered glass types, each serving distinct safety needs. Wired glass features a wire mesh to prevent shattering under heat. Ceramic glass offers high thermal resistance and withstands extreme temperatures, making it ideal for fire-rated openings. Laminated glass consists of layers bonded with interlayers, enhancing safety while resisting fire. Tempered glass is thermally treated for strength and durability, providing protection in various applications while remaining aesthetically pleasing. Each type serves specific fire safety requirements.</p></p>

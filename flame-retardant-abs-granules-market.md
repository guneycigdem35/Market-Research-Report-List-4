<p><p>Flame Retardant ABS Granules Market Types include Halogen Type and Halogen Free Type. Halogen Type flame retardants contain halogen compounds that release gases when exposed to high temperature, creating a barrier against combustion. However, they may produce toxic fumes. On the other hand, Halogen Free Type flame retardants use alternative compounds that do not release toxic gases when burned, making them more environmentally friendly. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages, catering to different needs in the market.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Side-Mount Floor Locks</li><li>Top-Mount Floor Locks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Floor locks are devices used to secure heavy equipment or machinery in place on a flat surface. There are two main types of floor locks - side-mount floor locks and top-mount floor locks. Side-mount floor locks are installed on the side of the equipment and use a lever to engage with the floor, while top-mount floor locks are installed on the top surface of the equipment and use a foot pedal or lever to engage with the floor. Both types provide stability and prevent the equipment from moving during operation.</p></p>

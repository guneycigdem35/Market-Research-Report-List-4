<p><p>Floor-standing automated biochemical analyzers are commonly categorized into two types: semi-automated and fully automated. Semi-automated analyzers require some manual intervention from laboratory technicians to load samples and reagents, while fully automated analyzers can perform all steps of the analysis process, from sample preparation to result reporting, without the need for human intervention. Semi-automated analyzers are typically more cost-effective and suitable for smaller labs, while fully automated analyzers are more efficient and suitable for high-throughput testing facilities.</p></p>

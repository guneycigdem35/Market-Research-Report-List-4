<p><p>Fungal Detection Kits are available in various types including those that utilize an antigen or antibody in a solid phase, an enzyme labeled antigen or antibody, and substrates on which enzymes act. Kits utilizing an antigen or antibody in a solid phase rely on the binding of fungal antigens or antibodies to detect and identify fungal infections. Kits with enzyme labeled antigens or antibodies use enzymes to amplify signals for more sensitive detection. Substrates on which enzymes act aid in the detection and quantification of fungal pathogens.</p></p>

<p><p>Gas lasers are categorized into different types based on their operating mode. Pulse gas lasers generate short bursts of high-intensity light, making them suitable for applications requiring precise and controlled energy delivery. Continuous gas lasers emit a continuous beam of light, ideal for tasks that require constant illumination. Apart from these two categories, other gas laser markets include tunable gas lasers, gas discharge lasers, and chemical lasers, each serving specific industry needs such as medical, industrial, and military applications.</p></p>

<p><p>Geared Elevator Market Types include Isokinetic Operation and Frequency Conversion systems. Isokinetic Operation involves a constant speed throughout the elevator's journey, providing smooth and consistent movement. On the other hand, Frequency Conversion allows for variable speeds, adjusting the elevator's speed based on demand and traffic patterns. Both market types cater to the specific needs of different buildings and users, offering efficient and reliable elevator services. They are commonly used in high-rise buildings, commercial complexes, and residential towers to enhance vertical transportation experiences.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Alkaline (ALK) Electrolyzer</li><li>Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Electrolyzer</li><li>Solid Oxide Electrolyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The green hydrogen market consists of three main electrolyzer types: Alkaline (ALK) electrolyzers, Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) electrolyzers, and Solid Oxide Electrolyzers. ALK electrolyzers use alkaline solutions for hydrogen production and are cost-effective for large-scale applications. PEM electrolyzers offer higher efficiency and fast response times, suitable for fluctuating renewable energy sources. Solid Oxide Electrolyzers operate at high temperatures, enabling greater efficiency in hydrogen production, especially when integrated with heat sources, making them promising for industrial applications.</p></p>

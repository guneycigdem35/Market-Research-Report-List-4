<p><p>Hall Effect Magnetic Proximity Sensors are crucial for detecting magnetic fields and are categorized mainly into two types: Threshold Type and Linear Type. Threshold Type sensors switch on or off at specific magnetic field strengths, ideal for applications requiring simple binary outputs. In contrast, Linear Type sensors provide a continuous output proportional to the magnetic field strength, allowing for precise measurements. These distinctions cater to varied industrial applications, from automotive systems to consumer electronics, enhancing functionality and control.</p></p>

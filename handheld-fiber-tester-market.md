<p><p>Handheld Fiber Testers are crucial tools in the telecom industry, with various types serving different purposes. Optical Power Meters measure the power of optical signals transmitted through fibers. Light Source Testers generate light signals for testing fiber optic cables. Optical Multimeters combine power meter and light source functions. OTDRs precisely measure the length and loss of fiber optic cables. Optical Fault Locators detect and locate faults in fiber optic cables. Other types may include Visual Fault Locators, Connectivity Testers, and Inspection Probes.</p></p>

<p><p>Hem flange bonding adhesives are available in two main types: one-component and two-component. One-component adhesives are ready to use and do not require mixing before application, making them convenient and easy to use. On the other hand, two-component adhesives consist of separate resin and hardener components that need to be mixed before application. While two-component adhesives may offer stronger bonding properties, they require more precise application techniques. Both types of adhesives cater to different preferences and requirements in the hem flange bonding adhesive market.</p></p>

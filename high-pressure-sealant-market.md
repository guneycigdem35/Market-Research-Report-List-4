<p><p>High Pressure Sealants are widely used in both hydraulic and pneumatic applications. On the other hand, Pneumatic High Pressure Sealants are specifically formulated to work in pneumatic systems, where air pressure is the driving force. Both types of sealants play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and effectiveness of high-pressure systems by preventing leaks and ensuring smooth operation.</p></p>

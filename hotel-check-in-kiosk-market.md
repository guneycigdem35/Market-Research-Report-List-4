<p><p>Hotel check-in kiosks come in two main types: wall-mounted and floor-standing. Wall-mounted kiosks are installed on the wall and are ideal for spaces with limited floor space. They are sleek and space-saving, making them perfect for smaller hotel lobbies. On the other hand, floor-standing kiosks are freestanding units that can be easily moved around. They are more visible and can attract attention from guests, making them suitable for larger hotel lobbies or areas with high foot traffic.</p></p>

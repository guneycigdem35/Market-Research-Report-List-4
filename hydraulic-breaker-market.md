<p><p>The Hydraulic Breaker market can be categorized into two main types: Handheld Hydraulic Breaker and Machine Mounted Hydraulic Breaker. Handheld Hydraulic Breakers are portable, versatile tools that are commonly used for smaller-scale demolition projects and in tight spaces where larger machinery cannot reach. Machine Mounted Hydraulic Breakers, on the other hand, are attached to larger construction equipment such as excavators or skid steers and are designed for heavier-duty applications like breaking rocks, concrete, and asphalt. Both types of Hydraulic Breakers play vital roles in the construction and demolition industries.</p></p>

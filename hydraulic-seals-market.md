<p><p>Hydraulic seals are essential components in hydraulic systems to prevent leakage and maintain the efficiency of the system. The market for hydraulic seals can be categorized into three main types: Hydraulic Piston Seals, which prevent fluid from leaking past the piston; Hydraulic Rod Seals, which prevent fluid from leaking along the rod; and Hydraulic Wiper Seals, which scrape dirt, moisture, and other contaminants from the rod to prevent damage to the system. Each type serves a specific function in maintaining the integrity and performance of hydraulic systems.</p></p>

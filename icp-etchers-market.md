<p><strong>The ICP Etchers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Load</li><li>Lock-Load</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ICP etchers come in different market types, such as open-load and lock-load. In open-load systems, the chamber is accessible from all sides, making it easier to load and unload samples. This type of etcher is ideal for research and development environments where frequent sample changes are needed. On the other hand, lock-load systems feature a sealed chamber that requires specific loading and unloading procedures. These systems are more suited for production environments where process consistency and contamination control are crucial.</p></p>

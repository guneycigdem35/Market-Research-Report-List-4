<p><p>Immersible ultrasonic transducers come in three main types: bottom vibration, side vibration, and top vibration markets. Bottom vibration transducers are designed to be positioned at the bottom of tanks or containers and emit ultrasonic waves upwards. Side vibration transducers are mounted on the sides of tanks and emit waves horizontally. Top vibration transducers are placed on the top of tanks and emit waves downwards. Each type of transducer is specialized for different applications and offers unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the user.</p></p>

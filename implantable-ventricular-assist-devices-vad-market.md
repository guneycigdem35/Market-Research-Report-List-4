<p><p>Implantable Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs) are mechanical pumps used to support heart function in patients with severe heart failure. The market is categorized into three main types: Left Ventricular Assist Devices (LVADs), which support the left ventricle; Right Ventricular Assist Devices (RVADs), which aid the right ventricle; and Biventricular Assist Devices (BIVADs), that assist both ventricles. These devices are crucial for prolonging survival and improving quality of life for individuals awaiting heart transplants or those who are not candidates for surgery.</p></p>

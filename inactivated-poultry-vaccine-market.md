<p><p>Inactivated poultry vaccines are available in two main types: liquid vaccines and freeze-dried vaccines. Liquid vaccines are ready-to-use formulations that can be directly administered to poultry. Freeze-dried vaccines, on the other hand, require reconstitution with a diluent before administration. Both types of vaccines are designed to stimulate the immune system of poultry without causing disease, providing protection against various poultry diseases. The choice between liquid and freeze-dried vaccines depends on factors such as ease of storage, transportation, and administration.</p></p>

<p><p>Industrial Infrared Scanners are used for various applications such as temperature measurement and thermal imaging. Standard Scanners utilize a single lens to capture infrared images, providing accurate temperature readings from a single perspective. On the other hand, Stereo Scanners use two lenses to capture images from different angles, enabling more precise depth perception and improved spatial awareness. Stereo scanners are often used in complex industrial environments where precise measurements are crucial for ensuring operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>

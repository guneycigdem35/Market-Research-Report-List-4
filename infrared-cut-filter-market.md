<p><strong>The Infrared Cut Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective Infrared Cut Filter</li><li>Absorption Infrared Cut Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared cut filter market can be categorized into two main types: reflective and absorption filters. Reflective infrared cut filters use reflective coatings to block infrared light while allowing visible light to pass through, enhancing image quality in cameras and optical devices. In contrast, absorption infrared cut filters function by absorbing infrared wavelengths, preventing them from interfering with the captured image. Both types serve vital roles in various applications, including photography, medical imaging, and optical instrumentation, ensuring accurate color representation and image clarity.</p></p>

<p><p>Insulated Metal Substrate (IMS) PCBs are specialized circuit boards designed to provide excellent thermal management in electronic devices. These substrates typically comprise a metal base, often aluminum or copper, coupled with an insulating layer and a dielectric material, which enhances thermal conduction while maintaining electrical insulation. The IMS technology is particularly favored in high-power applications, such as LED lighting, power electronics, and automotive systems, where heat dissipation is critical.</p><p>The IMS PCB market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the rising demand for efficient thermal management solutions across various sectors. The increasing integration of electronic components in transportation, consumer electronics, and industrial applications is fueling market expansion. Additionally, advancements in manufacturing processes and materials are enhancing the performance and reliability of IMS PCBs.</p><p>Emerging trends include a growing focus on miniaturization, leading to the development of compact and lightweight IMS solutions.

<p><p>Intelligent heated toilet seats come in two main types: tank type and tank-less type. Tank type seats are connected to a water tank, which heats the water before it is sent to the seat. These seats rely on a reservoir of pre-heated water, providing consistent warmth. In contrast, tank-less type seats heat the water instantly as it flows through, offering on-demand warmth. Both types offer different benefits, with tank type seats being more energy-efficient and tank-less type seats providing instant warmth.</p></p>

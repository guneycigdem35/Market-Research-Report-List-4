<p><strong>The Interchangeable Lens Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interchangeable Lens Cameras are classified based on the type of image sensor they use - CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) or CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors provide high-quality images with low noise but are more expensive and consume more power. On the other hand, CMOS sensors are cost-effective, power-efficient, and offer good image quality. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, catering to different market needs such as professionals preferring CCD for its image quality, while casual users opting for CMOS for its affordability.</p></p>

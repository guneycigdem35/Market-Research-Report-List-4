<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Laboratory Steam Sterilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Cycle</li><li>SFPP Cycle</li><li>Pre-Vac Cycle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laboratory steam sterilizers market includes various types tailored for different sterilization needs. The Gravity Cycle, which relies on gravity to displace air, is ideal for liquid and porous loads. The SFPP (Steam Flush Pressure Pulse) Cycle uses rapid steam pulses to enhance penetration, making it suitable for delicate instruments with complex geometries. The Pre-Vac Cycle employs a vacuum to eliminate air before steam infiltration, ensuring thorough sterilization of dense loads. Each cycle offers unique advantages for effective laboratory sterilization.</p></p>

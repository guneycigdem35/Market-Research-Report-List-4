<p><p>Laser Direct Imaging (LDI) systems are used in the printed circuit board industry for high precision imaging of circuit patterns. Two common types in the market are the Polygon Mirror 365nm and DMD 405nm systems. The Polygon Mirror 365nm system utilizes a rotating mirror to direct the laser beam, providing high speed and accuracy for imaging. On the other hand, the DMD 405nm system uses digital micromirror devices to modulate the laser beam, offering flexibility in creating intricate patterns. Both systems are essential tools for efficient PCB manufacturing.</p></p>

<p><p>LED headlamps are divided into two main market types: Rechargeable and Non-rechargeable. Rechargeable LED headlamps are equipped with a built-in battery that can be easily recharged using a USB cable or a charging dock. They are environmentally friendly and cost-effective in the long run. On the other hand, Non-rechargeable LED headlamps require disposable batteries that need to be replaced regularly. While they may be more convenient for occasional use, they can be less cost-effective and create more waste.</p></p>

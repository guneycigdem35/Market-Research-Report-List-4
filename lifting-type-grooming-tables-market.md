<p><p>Lifting type grooming tables come in two main types: electric and hydraulic. Electric grooming tables are operated by a motor that lifts and lowers the table with the push of a button, making them convenient and easy to use. On the other hand, hydraulic grooming tables use a hydraulic pump to adjust the height manually by pumping a lever. Both types offer stability and safety for pets during grooming sessions, catering to the needs of different groomers and their preferences.</p></p>

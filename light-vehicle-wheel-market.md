Forging involves shaping heated metal under immense pressure to enhance the wheel's strength and durability. The "Other" market includes alternative manufacturing methods such as flow forming and composite materials, offering unique benefits in terms of weight reduction and performance. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the light vehicle wheel market.</p></p>

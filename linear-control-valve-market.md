<p><ul><li>Gate Valves</li><li>Diaphragm Valves</li><li>Ball Valves</li><li>Butterfly Valves</li><li>Plug Valves</li><li>Other Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear control valve market consists of various types of valves such as gate valves, diaphragm valves, ball valves, butterfly valves, plug valves, and other valves. Gate valves are commonly used for on/off control applications, while diaphragm valves are suitable for applications requiring good sealing and cleanliness. Ball valves are versatile and can be used for both on/off and throttling applications. Butterfly valves are cost-effective and have low pressure drops. Plug valves are reliable and offer tight shut-off capabilities. Other valves include globe valves, pinch valves, and needle valves.</p></p>

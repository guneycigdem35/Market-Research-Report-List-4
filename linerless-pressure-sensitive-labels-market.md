<p><p>Linerless pressure-sensitive labels are self-adhesive labels that do not have a release liner. These labels are applied directly onto the product without the need for a backing material, resulting in reduced waste and improved efficiency in labeling processes. They are widely used in various industries such as food and beverage, retail, logistics, and pharmaceuticals.</p><p>The linerless pressure-sensitive labels market is expected to experience significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 12.8% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for sustainable packaging solutions, as linerless labels help reduce environmental impact by eliminating backing paper waste. Additionally, the growing trend towards convenient and easy-to-use labeling solutions is driving the adoption of linerless pressure-sensitive labels.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in label printing technologies such as improved adhesives and application systems are contributing to the market growth. Manufacturers are also innovating with features like repositionable adhesives and enhanced print quality to meet the evolving needs of end-users. Overall, the linerless pressure-sensitive labels market is poised for continued expansion in the coming years.</p></p>

<p><p>The lubrication system market can be divided into two main types: manual lubrication systems and automatic lubrication systems. Manual lubrication systems require operators to manually apply lubricants to machinery at scheduled intervals, whereas automatic lubrication systems deliver lubricants to machinery automatically based on predetermined settings. Manual systems are cost-effective but require more labor, while automatic systems are efficient and ensure consistent lubrication but come with a higher initial investment. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring smooth functioning and longevity of machinery.</p></p>

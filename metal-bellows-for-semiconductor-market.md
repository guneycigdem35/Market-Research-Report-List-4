<p><p>Metal bellows are crucial components in the semiconductor market, designed to accommodate movement and pressure changes in vacuum systems. Edge welded metal bellows are created by joining metal sheets at the edges, offering high flexibility and durability, suitable for applications with large pressure differentials. Conversely, electroformed metal bellows are produced through an electroforming process, resulting in thinner walls and precise dimensions, ideal for applications requiring tight tolerances and high responsiveness. Both types enhance system reliability and performance in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>

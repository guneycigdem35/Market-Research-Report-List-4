<p><p>Microwave dielectric ceramics can be categorized into three types based on their dielectric constant: Low Dielectric Constant Type, Intermediary Dielectric Constant Type, and High Dielectric Constant Type. The Low Dielectric Constant Type ceramics have a low dielectric constant, making them suitable for applications requiring high-frequency stability and low signal loss. The Intermediary Dielectric Constant Type ceramics have a moderate dielectric constant, balancing performance between low and high types. The High Dielectric Constant Type ceramics have a high dielectric constant, ideal for applications requiring high capacitance.</p></p>

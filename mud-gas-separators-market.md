<p><ul><li>Closed bottom type</li><li>Open bottom type</li><li>Float type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mud gas separators are used in the drilling industry to separate gas from drilling mud, preventing dangerous blowouts. There are three main types of separators on the market. Closed bottom type separators have a sealed bottom, preventing gas from escaping. Open bottom type separators allow gas to escape from the bottom. Float type separators use a floating device to control the level of the liquid inside. Each type has its own applications and benefits, catering to different drilling conditions and requirements in the industry.</p></p>

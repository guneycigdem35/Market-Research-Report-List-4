<p><p>Nanotechnology drug delivery market involves various types such as nanocrystals, nanoparticles, liposomes, micelles, nanotubes, and others. Nanocrystals are solid particles with diameters in the nanometer range, while nanoparticles are small particles typically used to deliver drugs to specific targets. Liposomes are lipid-based vesicles used to encapsulate drugs, and micelles are aggregates of amphiphilic molecules. Nanotubes are cylindrical nanostructures used for drug delivery. Other types of drug delivery systems in the nanotechnology market include dendrimers, quantum dots, and nanorods.</p></p>

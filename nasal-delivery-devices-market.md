<p><p>Nasal delivery devices can be categorized into three main types based on dose administration: unit-dose, bi-dose, and multi-dose. Unit-dose devices deliver a single predetermined dose of medication per administration. Bi-dose devices deliver two predetermined doses per administration, usually for both nostrils. Multi-dose devices are designed to deliver multiple doses over a period of time, allowing patients to self-administer medication as prescribed. Each type has its own advantages and applicability in different medical situations.</p></p>

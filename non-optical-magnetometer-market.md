<p><ul><li>Absolute Magnetometer</li><li>Relative Magnetometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-optical magnetometer market can be classified into two types: Absolute Magnetometers and Relative Magnetometers. Absolute magnetometers provide accurate measurements of the Earth's magnetic field, allowing for precise navigation and orientation in various applications. On the other hand, relative magnetometers measure changes in magnetic fields relative to a baseline, making them suitable for tasks such as detecting anomalies or monitoring magnetic variations. Both types of magnetometers serve different purposes and cater to diverse industries such as aerospace, defense, and geophysical exploration.</p></p>

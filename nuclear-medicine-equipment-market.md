<p><ul><li>Hybrid SPECT</li><li>Standalone SPECT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nuclear Medicine Equipment Market is segmented into two main types: Hybrid SPECT and Standalone SPECT. Hybrid SPECT combines imaging technologies like CT or MRI with SPECT for more accurate and detailed results. Standalone SPECT, on the other hand, solely focuses on SPECT imaging. Hybrid SPECT is preferred for complex cases requiring both anatomical and functional information, while Standalone SPECT is more cost-effective and simpler to use, making it suitable for routine imaging procedures. Both types play significant roles in the nuclear medicine equipment market.</p></p>

<p><p>Occupant Sensing System (OSS) is a safety feature in vehicles that detects the presence of passengers in the vehicle seats, enabling the activation of airbags and other safety systems in the event of a crash. This technology uses sensors to determine the weight and positioning of occupants, ensuring that airbags deploy with the appropriate force to protect passengers.</p><p>The global Occupant Sensing System Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period, with a projected CAGR of 11.1%.

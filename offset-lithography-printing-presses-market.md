<p><strong>The Offset Lithography Printing Presses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sheet-Fed Offset Printing Press</li><li>Web-Fed Offset Printing Press</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offset lithography printing presses are divided into two main types: sheet-fed offset printing press and web-fed offset printing press. Sheet-fed offset printing press is designed for printing on individual sheets of paper, making it suitable for smaller print jobs and personalization. Web-fed offset printing press, on the other hand, is used for high-volume printing on continuous rolls of paper, making it more cost-effective for larger print runs. Both types of presses use the offset printing process, where ink is transferred from a plate to a rubber blanket before being pressed onto the paper.</p></p>

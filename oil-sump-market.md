<p><p>The oil sump market can be divided into two main types: structural and non-structural. Structural oil sumps are an integral part of the engine's design and provide structural support to other engine components. Non-structural oil sumps, on the other hand, are standalone components that solely serve the purpose of storing and distributing oil throughout the engine. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring proper lubrication and cooling of the engine, ultimately affecting its performance and longevity.</p></p>

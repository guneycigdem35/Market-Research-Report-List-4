<p><p>The Ophthalmic Femtosecond Laser System market can be categorized into two types: Full Femtosecond and Half Femtosecond systems. Full Femtosecond systems utilize femtosecond lasers for all steps of the procedure, including corneal flap creation and lens fragmentation. In contrast, Half Femtosecond systems combine femtosecond laser technology with other traditional surgical tools for certain parts of the procedure. Both types offer precision and improved outcomes in ophthalmic surgeries, with Full Femtosecond systems being favored for their all-in-one approach.</p></p>

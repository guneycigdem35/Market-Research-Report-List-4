<p><p>Optical scales are widely used in industries for precise measurement and position control. Incremental optical scales provide high accuracy by measuring the displacement or movement of an object incrementally. Absolute optical scales, on the other hand, have the ability to determine the exact position of an object without the need for a reference point. Both types of optical scales have their own market, with incremental scales being commonly used in applications requiring accurate positioning feedback and absolute scales being preferred for applications where precise positioning is crucial.</p></p>

<p><p>Paraffin inhibitors for crude oil can be classified into three main types: Polymer type, Surfactant type, and Compound type. Polymer type inhibitors work by forming a protective layer on the surface of the equipment, preventing paraffin buildup. Surfactant type inhibitors reduce the surface tension of the oil, making it easier to flow through pipelines. Compound type inhibitors combine the properties of both polymers and surfactants, providing a comprehensive solution to paraffin deposition in crude oil production and transportation.</p></p>

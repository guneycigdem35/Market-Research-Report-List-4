<p><p>PE-RT Pipes Market is categorized into two types: PE-RT Ⅰ and PE-RT Ⅱ. PE-RT Ⅰ pipes are designed for use in low-temperature applications, such as underfloor heating systems. They have lower flexibility compared to PE-RT Ⅱ pipes, which are ideal for high-temperature applications like hot water distribution. PE-RT Ⅰ pipes have better long-term pressure resistance, while PE-RT Ⅱ pipes offer superior resistance to high temperatures. Both types of pipes are commonly used in plumbing and heating systems for residential and commercial buildings.</p></p>

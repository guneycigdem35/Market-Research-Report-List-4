<p><p>Photomasks for semiconductor market include EUV mask, UV mask, and DUV mask types. EUV (extreme ultraviolet) mask is used for advanced lithography processes in the semiconductor industry. UV (ultraviolet) masks are used for various applications in photolithography, while DUV (deep ultraviolet) masks are used for high-resolution imaging. Each type of mask plays a crucial role in the production of semiconductor products by enabling precise patterning on silicon wafers. These masks help in creating intricate circuit patterns that are essential for the functionality of semiconductor devices.</p></p>

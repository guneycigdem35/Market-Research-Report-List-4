<p><p>The photoresistor market encompasses several types, primarily categorized by the wavelength of light they respond to. UV Light Dependent Resistors (ULDRs) are sensitive to ultraviolet light, making them ideal for safety and environmental applications. Infrared Photosensitive Resistors (IPRs) detect infrared radiation, commonly used in night vision and thermal sensors. Visible Light Dependent Resistors (VLDs) are responsive to the visible spectrum and are widely used in lighting control. Additionally, the "Other" category includes specialized resistors designed for niche applications, adapting to specific needs.</p></p>

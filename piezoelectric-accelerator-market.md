<p><p>Piezoelectric accelerometers, including linear and pendulous types, are commonly used in various industries for measuring acceleration. Linear accelerometers are designed to measure acceleration along a single axis, providing precise and accurate readings for applications where linear motion is important. Pendulous accelerometers, on the other hand, utilize a pendulum mechanism to measure acceleration in multiple directions, making them ideal for use in applications where multi-axis motion needs to be monitored. Both types of accelerometers play a crucial role in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial manufacturing.</p></p>

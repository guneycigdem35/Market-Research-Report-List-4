<p><p>Pine tar is classified primarily into two market types: industrial grade and medical grade. Industrial-grade pine tar is predominantly used in applications such as roofing, wood preservation, and as an ingredient in soaps and detergents, serving the construction and manufacturing sectors. Conversely, medical-grade pine tar is utilized in dermatological products for its therapeutic properties, particularly in treating conditions like psoriasis and eczema. Both grades derive from the distillation of pine wood, but their purity and intended use significantly differ.</p></p>

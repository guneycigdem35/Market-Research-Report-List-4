There are two main types of pneumatic nebulizers in the market - vented pneumatic nebulizers and breath-actuated pneumatic nebulizers. Vented pneumatic nebulizers deliver medication continuously, while breath-actuated nebulizers only release medication when the user inhales. Both types are effective in delivering medication to the lungs and are commonly used in the treatment of respiratory conditions such as asthma and COPD.</p></p>

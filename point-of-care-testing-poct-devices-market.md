<p><p>Point-of-care testing (POCT) devices market includes various types of equipment used for on-the-spot medical testing. Urinalysis analyzers are used to analyze urine samples, while blood glucose meters are used for monitoring blood sugar levels. Clinical chemistry analyzers are used for analyzing various biochemical substances in blood. Blood gas analyzers measure the levels of oxygen and carbon dioxide in the blood. INR test meters are used for monitoring the effectiveness of blood-thinning medications. Cardiac marker analyzers are used for diagnosing heart conditions. PH meters measure the acidity or alkalinity of a sample. Electrolyte analyzers measure the levels of electrolytes in the blood. Hemoglobin meters are used for testing hemoglobin levels. Other types of POCT devices include coagulation analyzers, cholesterol analyzers, and drug testing devices.</p></p>

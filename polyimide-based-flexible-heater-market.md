<p><strong>The Polyimide-based Flexible Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyimide flexible heater (no backing)</li><li>Aluminum / Copper Plated Polyimide Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyimide-based flexible heaters come in two main types: Polyimide flexible heaters without any backing material, and Aluminum/Copper plated Polyimide heaters. The Polyimide flexible heaters without backing are lightweight and thin, making them ideal for applications where space is limited. On the other hand, Aluminum/Copper plated Polyimide heaters offer faster heat transfer and better thermal conductivity due to the metal plating on the surface. Both types of heaters are versatile and commonly used in industries such as electronics, aerospace, and medical devices.</p></p>

<p><p>Powder bed fusion technology includes various types such as Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS), Selective Heat Sintering (SHS), Selective Laser Sintering (SLS), Electron Beam Melting (EBM), and Selective Laser Melting (SLM). DMLS uses a high-powered laser to selectively fuse metal powder together. SHS uses infrared lamps to heat and fuse powdered material. SLS involves using a laser to sinter powdered material together. EBM uses an electron beam to melt metal powder, while SLM uses a laser to selectively melt and fuse metal powders.</p></p>

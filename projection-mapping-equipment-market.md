<p><p>Projection mapping equipment market is divided into two main types: DLP (digital light processing) and LCD (liquid crystal display) technologies. </p><p>DLP projectors use thousands of tiny mirrors to reflect light and produce high-quality, sharp images with vibrant colors. They are popular for their reliability and long lifespan, making them a preferred choice for large-scale events and installations. </p><p>On the other hand, LCD projectors use liquid crystal panels to create images. They are known for their affordability and easy maintenance, making them suitable for smaller-scale applications and presentations.</p></p>

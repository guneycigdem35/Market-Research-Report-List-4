<p><p>The rail wheel axle market consists of two main types: rolled axles and forged axles. Rolled axles are made by hot rolling steel into the desired shape, resulting in a cost-effective and durable product. On the other hand, forged axles are created by applying heat and pressure to shape the steel, producing a high-strength and more precise axle. Both types play a crucial role in the transportation industry, providing reliable support for rail vehicles and ensuring safe and efficient operation.</p></p>

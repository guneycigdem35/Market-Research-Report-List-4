<p><p>Recombinant Protein Market Types include Molecular Cloning and Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR). Molecular Cloning involves the insertion of a specific gene into a vector and then transferring it into a host organism for protein expression. On the other hand, PCR is a technique used to amplify a specific region of DNA through repeated cycles of denaturation, annealing, and extension. Both methods are essential in the production and study of recombinant proteins for various applications in biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, and research.</p></p>

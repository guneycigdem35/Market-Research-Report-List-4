<p><strong>The Reptile Incubator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circulated Air Incubators</li><li>Still Air Incubators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reptile incubators come in two main types: circulated air and still air. Circulated air incubators have fans that help distribute heat and maintain a consistent temperature throughout the entire unit, ensuring that eggs are evenly heated and hatched. Still air incubators, on the other hand, rely on natural airflow and heat distribution within the unit, which can sometimes lead to uneven temperatures and less successful hatching rates. Both types are commonly used in the reptile breeding industry, with circulated air incubators generally being preferred for their more reliable results.</p></p>

<p><p>Single spindle lathes have two main market types - vertical CNC lathes and horizontal CNC lathes. Vertical CNC lathes are designed with the cutting tool mounted vertically, allowing for easy chip removal and better visibility of the machining process. Horizontal CNC lathes, on the other hand, have the cutting tool mounted horizontally, providing better stability for heavy-duty machining operations. Both types of lathes are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics for precision metal cutting and shaping.</p></p>

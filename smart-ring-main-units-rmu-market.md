Solid Insulated RMUs use solid dielectric materials to provide insulation, while Gas Insulated RMUs use sulfur hexafluoride gas for insulation. Air Insulated RMUs rely on air for insulation. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications based on factors such as cost, space, and environmental impact.</p></p>

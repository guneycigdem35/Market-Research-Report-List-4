<p><p>Smart stethoscopes come in two main types: wireless stethoscopes and stethoscopes with wires. Wireless stethoscopes utilize Bluetooth technology to connect to smartphones or other devices, allowing for easier data storage and analysis. On the other hand, stethoscopes with wires are more traditional in design, with a direct connection to the listening device. Both types offer advanced features such as noise cancellation and digital sound amplification, improving the quality of auscultation for healthcare professionals.</p></p>

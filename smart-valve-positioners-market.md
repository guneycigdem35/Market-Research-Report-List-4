<p><p>Smart valve positioners are devices that control the position of a valve actuator based on a control signal. There are two main types in the market: Single Acting Positioners and Double Acting Positioners. Single Acting Positioners utilize air pressure to move a valve actuator in one direction, while Double Acting Positioners use air pressure to move the valve actuator in both directions. Both types are essential in industries where precise valve control is required for efficient and safe operations.</p></p>

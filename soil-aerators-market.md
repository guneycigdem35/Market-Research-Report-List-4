Mechanical soil aerators use rotating spikes or blades to penetrate the soil and create holes, while pneumatic soil aerators use pressurized air to blast holes into the soil. Both types of aerators have their own advantages and are important tools for maintaining healthy soil and promoting plant growth.</p></p>

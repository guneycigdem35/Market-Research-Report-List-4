<p><p>Solar Photovoltaic (PV) Cells and Modules market consists of different types such as Passivated Emitter Rear Cell (PERC), Tunnel Oxide Passivated Contact (TOPCon), Heterojunction Technology (HJT), Heterojunction Interdigitated Back Contact (IBC), and others. PERC technology enhances cell efficiency by improving rear side passivation. TOPCon technology uses tunnel oxide layers for better electron transmission. HJT technology combines crystalline silicon with thin-film materials for higher efficiency. IBC technology reduces energy loss through interdigitated back contacts. Other technologies also focus on maximizing energy generation and efficiency.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Solid Pneumatic Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3-Stage</li><li>2-Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid pneumatic tires market can be segmented based on their design into 3-stage and 2-stage types. 3-stage tires have a three-layer construction, with a solid inner core surrounded by two layers of pneumatic material. This design offers increased durability and stability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, 2-stage tires have a two-layer construction, with a solid inner core and a single layer of pneumatic material. They are better suited for lighter applications and provide a balance between comfort and durability.</p></p>

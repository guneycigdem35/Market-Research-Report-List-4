<p><ul><li>In-line type</li><li>Elbow type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Speed control valves are used to regulate the flow rate of fluid in hydraulic systems. There are two main types of speed control valves in the market: in-line type and elbow type. In-line type valves are installed directly in the hydraulic line for straightforward flow control. Elbow type valves, on the other hand, are designed with a 90-degree angle for easier installation in tight spaces or when the hydraulic system requires a change in direction. Both types provide precise control over the speed and direction of fluid flow in hydraulic systems.</p></p>

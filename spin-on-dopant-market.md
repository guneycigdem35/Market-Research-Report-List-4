<p><p>The Spin-on Dopant market can be divided into two main types: P Type and N Type. P Type dopants are used to introduce positively charged ions into a semiconductor material, while N Type dopants introduce negatively charged ions. P Type dopants are typically used to create holes in the semiconductor material, while N Type dopants are used to create free electrons. Both types of dopants play a crucial role in controlling the conductivity and other electrical properties of semiconductor materials.</p></p>

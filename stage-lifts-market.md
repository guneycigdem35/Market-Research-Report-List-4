<p><p>Stage lifts market can be categorized into two main types: scissor lifts and multiple stage lift tables. Multiple stage lift tables consist of multiple interconnected platforms that can be adjusted independently, allowing for more precise positioning and versatility. Both types of lifts are commonly used in theaters, concert venues, and other performance spaces to elevate equipment, performers, or scenery during productions.</p></p>

<p><p>The stainless steel anchor chain market is primarily divided into two main types: marine anchor chains and offshore mooring chains. Marine anchor chains are used to secure vessels at anchorage, providing stability and safety in various water conditions. Offshore mooring chains, on the other hand, are designed for permanent or semi-permanent anchoring of floating structures, such as oil rigs and ships, ensuring they remain fixed in position despite harsh marine environments. Each type offers specific benefits suited to their applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The Surveillance Optronics market includes two types of technologies, namely multispectral and hyperspectral imaging. Multispectral imaging is the process of capturing data at various wavelengths across the electromagnetic spectrum, allowing for the detection of patterns and characteristics not visible to the human eye. Hyperspectral imaging, on the other hand, goes a step further by capturing hundreds of narrow spectral bands, providing more detailed and specific information about objects in the scene. Both technologies are revolutionizing surveillance and intelligence gathering in various industries.</p></p>

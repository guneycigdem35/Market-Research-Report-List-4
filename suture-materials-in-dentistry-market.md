<p><p>Suture materials used in dentistry market are divided into two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable sutures. Absorbable sutures are designed to break down and be absorbed by the body over time, eliminating the need for removal. Non-absorbable sutures, on the other hand, are made of materials that do not break down and must be removed manually after the wound has healed. Dentists choose the type of suture based on factors such as the location of the wound and the length of time sutures are needed.</p></p>

<p><p>The Triple Insulated Wire Market includes two main types: Standard Type and Self Bonding Type. Standard Type wires are designed with a layer of insulation between each conductor, providing excellent protection against electrical faults. Self Bonding Type wires have an additional self-bonding layer that eliminates the need for external adhesives during installation, making them easier to work with. Both types offer high thermal and electrical performance, making them suitable for a wide range of applications in various industries.</p></p>

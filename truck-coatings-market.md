<p><p>Truck coatings market includes various types of coatings such as Electrodeposition Coating, Primer, and Top Coat. Electrodeposition Coating is a process in which a positively charged paint is attracted to a negatively charged truck body to provide uniform coverage. Primers are used to enhance adhesion and corrosion resistance, while Top Coats provide the final finish and protection against wear, weathering, and UV rays. Together, these coatings help to improve the durability and aesthetic appeal of trucks.</p></p>

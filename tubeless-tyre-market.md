<p><ul><li>Radial Tubeless Tyre</li><li>Bias Tubeless Tyre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tubeless tyres are categorized into two main types: radial tubeless tyres and bias tubeless tyres. Radial tubeless tyres are constructed with fabric plies that run radially from bead to bead, providing greater stability and improved performance. Bias tubeless tyres, on the other hand, have fabric plies that run diagonally across the tyre, giving them a more flexible sidewall. Both types offer advantages such as reduced rolling resistance, better fuel efficiency, improved handling, and enhanced safety on the road.</p></p>

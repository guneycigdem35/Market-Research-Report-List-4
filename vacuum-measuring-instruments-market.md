<p><p>Vacuum Measuring Instruments are categorized into Low Vacuum and Medium-High Vacuum markets based on the pressure range they can handle. Low Vacuum instruments are designed for measuring pressure ranges up to 1x10^-3 Torr, typically used in applications such as leak detection and gas analysis. In contrast, Medium-High Vacuum instruments are capable of measuring pressure ranges from 1x10^-3 Torr to 1x10^-8 Torr. These instruments are commonly used in industries like semiconductor manufacturing and aerospace for more precise vacuum measurements.</p></p>

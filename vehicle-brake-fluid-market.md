<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li><li>DOT 5.1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle brake fluid market comprises primarily four types: DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1. DOT 3 is glycol-based, commonly used in most vehicles, with a boiling point of 401°F. DOT 4, also glycol-based, offers higher boiling points and is suitable for high-performance vehicles. DOT 5 is silicone-based, non-hygroscopic, and used in specific applications. DOT 5.1, like DOT 4, is glycol-based but has a higher boiling point, making it ideal for extreme conditions.</p></p>

<p><p>Vehicle Inertial Sensor Market includes three main types of sensors: Acceleration Sensor, Gyroscope, and IMU (Inertial Measurement Unit). Acceleration sensors measure the rate of change of velocity of a vehicle, providing crucial data for stability control and impact detection. Gyroscopes measure the vehicle's angular velocity and aid in navigation and stabilization systems. IMUs combine both acceleration and gyroscope sensors to provide comprehensive data on the vehicle's position, orientation, and movements, making them essential for applications like autonomous driving and driver assistance systems.</p></p>

<p><p>The vessel fleet management software market can be categorized into two main types: onshore-based and vessel-based. Onshore-based systems are operated from a central location on land, allowing for remote monitoring and management of vessel activities. Vessel-based systems, on the other hand, are installed directly on the vessels themselves, providing real-time data and connectivity while at sea. Both types offer solutions for optimizing fleet operations, maintenance scheduling, and performance monitoring to improve efficiency and reduce costs in the maritime industry.</p></p>

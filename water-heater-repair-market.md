<p><p>The water heater repair market can be divided into two main types: tank storage and tankless water heaters. Tank storage water heaters are the traditional type that store and heat water in a tank until it is needed. Tankless water heaters, on the other hand, heat water on demand without the need for a storage tank, providing hot water instantly. Both markets cater to different consumer needs and preferences, with tankless water heaters becoming increasingly popular due to their energy efficiency and space-saving benefits.</p></p>

<p><p>Whole Blood Coagulation Analyzers (WBCAs) are used to assess the blood's ability to clot, crucial for diagnosis and monitoring of various diseases. These analyzers are available in three main types - Standalone Analysers, Table Top Analysers, and Portable Analysers. Standalone Analysers are larger, high-throughput machines suitable for centralized labs. Table Top Analysers offer medium throughput and are ideal for smaller labs. Portable Analysers are compact, easy to use devices that provide quick results, making them ideal for point-of-care testing in remote locations.</p></p>

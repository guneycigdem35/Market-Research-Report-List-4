<p><strong>The Wired Aviation Headset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Noise Reduction (PNR) Headsets</li><li>Active Noise Reduction (ANR) Headsets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive Noise Reduction (PNR) Headsets use soundproofing materials to block out external noise, providing a quieter environment for the user. They are more affordable and have simpler technology. Active Noise Reduction (ANR) Headsets use advanced technology to cancel out external noise through phase-reversal techniques, creating a quieter and more comfortable listening experience. While ANR headsets are more expensive, they offer superior noise-canceling capabilities and are preferred by professionals who spend long hours in noisy aircraft environments.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Sensor</li><li>Micromechanical Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The yaw sensor market is primarily divided into two types: piezoelectric sensors and micromechanical sensors. Piezoelectric sensors leverage the piezoelectric effect to detect rotational motion, providing high sensitivity and quick response times, making them ideal for dynamic applications. In contrast, micromechanical sensors incorporate microelectromechanical systems (MEMS) technology, allowing for compact designs and mass production. They offer durability and robustness while ensuring precise measurements, making both sensor types essential for applications in automotive, aerospace, and robotics industries.</p></p>
